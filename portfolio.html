Merchant Bug Tracker

This was an internal product purpose-built at Shopify to address a very significant problem, which was tracking and managing product defects reported by merchants through to resolution.

Problem

Every month, hundreds of product defects raised by merchants and validated by escalated support we reported. Over half of these went to the wrong teams' GitHub repo (of which there were over 12,000), and very few of the rest were resolved in a time manner.

Solution

Over the course of four months, myself and three engineers designed, prototyped and built a new service from the ground up. We had the research to understand the underlying problem, but undertook more research to understand the users' workflows and jobs to be done, both on the support (those reporting bugs) and R&D (those responsible for fixing bugs) side.

After laying out the clear vision and goal definition, we created a tech design and circulate for feedback amongst many stakeholders and engineers. We created a prototype against the tech design over one sprint to get more feedback, and then torched it. We then had everything to build and ship a solid solution within the next month.

Impact

When we shipped this new product, Support agents had a clear and intuitive UI to report bugs, automation ensure they were assigned to the right team and matching GitHub issues open in the right place, R&D teams had notifications and new SLAs helping them to resolution, and we finally had a data set for reporting that was complete and accurate. Bug resolution within SLA times rose to +80%, and leadership had a clear picture of product quality health.
